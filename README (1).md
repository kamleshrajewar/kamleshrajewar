## About

Results-driven DevOps Engineer with 5+ years of experience deploying and managing cloud infrastructure on AWS and Azure, building automated CI/CD pipelines, and ensuring high availability of production systems. Hands-on with Terraform, Docker, Kubernetes, Ansible, and Jenkins — with a strong focus on monitoring, incident response, and cost-optimized, secure infrastructure.

Currently at **Tata Consultancy Services**, Pune, working as an internal DevOps consultant guiding teams through automated deployment pipelines and Terraform-based provisioning.
